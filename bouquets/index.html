Bouquets

I love making these gorgeous bunches each week. Why not use them to send a special message or treat yourself! I use locally sourced and seasonal flowers wrapped in recyclable craft paper. Bunches start from £25 and can either be collected from me on a Friday or Saturday or I have a delivery service for the Bristol BS7 area which costs £3 extra and operates on the Saturday. I sometimes offer jam jars bursting with bright colours for just £12.99. Keep an eye on my Facebook or Instagram pages to see when these are popping up.
