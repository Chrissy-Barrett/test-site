Weddings

I fell in love with floristry whilst planning and arranging my own wedding flowers, but I know when you first start there are so many things to think about and it can feel really overwhelming. That is where I come in. I am happy to discuss your ideas and help with seasonal flower suggestions and bouquet types. My style is generally wild and colourful but I am happy to work to other briefs. Currently I only offer bridal and bridesmaid bouquets, buttonholes, corsages and flower crowns. I know how stressful pricing can feel so below is a costing table to help you plan.
