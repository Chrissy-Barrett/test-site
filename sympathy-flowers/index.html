Sympathy Flowers

I am always honored to be asked to make a tribute to someone who has passed away. It is a very peaceful process and I take time thinking of the person I am making it for. Flowers really are so versatile and can convey lots of different emotions. I am really proud of my end of life wreaths, they can be made completely biodegradable and in any colour or style you wish

End of Life Wreaths

Some of my Wreaths pictured were in celebration of someones life, whiles others were based on their favorite colours or flowers. I think you’ll agree they are a beautiful way to pay your respects. If you’re looking for something that will last longer I can also make wreaths and bouquets with dried flowers.

I generally work with 12″ metal or wicca rings, applying moss as the base to keep the flowers healthy and then building the design upon that.

My wreaths start from £65 each, please get in touch if you’d like to discuss an order or design.

Sheathes and Bouquets

Another service I provide, which has proven very popular, is a bouquet made from smaller bunches that can be separated and taken home by different family members or friends. The cost on this arrangement really depends on how many small handties you require so just drop me a message.

I can provide Sheathes ranging in size with any colour theme. Please get in contact to discuss pricing and any other requirements
