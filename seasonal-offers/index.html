Christmas Wreaths & Kits

Christmas Wreath Kits

It is a strange time for us all right now. I would love to be running Christmas workshops to spread the festive joy! That may not be possible this year but that doesn’t mean I can’t still do my best to let you, and your families, have fun building and creating your own wreaths and table decorations (I love doing this face to face with a cheeky mulled wine but alas!). So instead, I am offering Christmas Wreaths Kits which can be posted anywhere in England.

They have everything you need to make a wreath, minus the foliage, which you can easily collect from your garden or on a walk with family and friends.

Below you will find a detailed list of what you will find inside your kit and there will also be a how to video available, for your support and amusement! They cost £19.99 plus postage…

Local to the Bristol area I am also offering the kit with foliage this costs £26, you have to collect from me in Ashley Down, Bristol.

Inside a Wreath Kit you’ll find

  • Metal ring frame
  • Reel of wire or twine
  • Moss
  • A spray bottle to keep your moss moist
  • Straight wires to attach decorations
  • Natural decorations: Cinnamon sticks (with twine to tie), Dried orange slices, fir cones and star anise
  • A Luxury bow
  • Paper instructions
  • A link to my how-to video

Luxury Wreaths

You can buy a pre-made Luxury wreaths from £35. I generally keep my decorations natural with all the Christmas classics and use a range of foliages, but I can also discuss something with a little more christmas bling. Sadly, I can not post these so its collection from Ashley Down, Bristol or delivery to BS7.

Festive centre piece

This year I am offering Christmas table decorations to adorn your table whilst you celebrate the festive season. They come bursting with foliage and classic christmas decorations and a large candle in the centre to create a warmth during the festive period. These are available from the 15th of December and cost £30.
